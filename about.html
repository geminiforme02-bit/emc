Our story

Two culinary grads, one tiny Kozhikode kitchen, and a stubborn belief that a burger is worth doing properly.

How it started

It began with fried chicken and a queue that wouldn't quit.

In 2020, Junaid Jabbar and Krishnapriya Hariharan started frying Nashville-style chicken out of a small kitchen in Kozhikode. Word travelled fast. What began as a passion project quickly turned into one of the city's most-loved burger spots.

Today, EMC is still run the same way — ingredients prepped fresh, patties smashed to order, and burgers built only when you ask for them. Nothing sits, nothing waits.

What "serious" means

No shortcuts. Just better burgers.

Fresh, never frozen

Ingredients prepped daily. If it isn't fresh, it doesn't go on the bun.

Flame-grilled

Real heat and char on every patty. You can taste the difference.

Smashed to order

Built only when you order, so it lands hot, juicy and at its best.

Named after legends

Newton, Albert, El Chapo… burgers with a sense of humour and serious flavour.
